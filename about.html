What's Butikk?

We're an online fashion community, focused on discussing affordable and stylish clothing.

We believe that fashion has evolved its exclusivity and microbrands are now taking over the space.

These microbrands however, despite having affordable and stylish clothing, don't have the big marketing budget that established brands have.
